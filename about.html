About Us

Mission

Yonder Deep provides students with experiences that allow them to make positive change and become tomorrow’s innovators. The team pursues oceanographic research, fostering skills in engineering and robotics. Collaborations with Dr. Grant Deane at Scripps Institution of Oceanography provide expertise and deployment opportunities for Yonder Deep’s technology.

Our Goals

Yonder Deep empowers students at UC San Diego through underwater robotics for oceanographic research and lifelong learning.
