Recruitment

At Yonder Deep, we value personal development above all else. Every member will have access to 3D printers, and a wealth of knowledge from advisors, instructors, and professors. Through workshops and rapid prototyping, members will quickly develop a multidisciplinary set of skills across the engineering spectrum. Our members have gone to intern and work at major companies like Amazon, HP, L3 Harris, Meta, NASA, and Northrop Grumman.

Process

To join our team, please submit an application form during our recruitment season.

Once submitted, we will review your application and contact you to set up an interview if you are a good fit.

After interviews are conducted, we will send out decisions the following week.
